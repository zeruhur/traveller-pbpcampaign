A Timeline

Year One corresponds to the foundation of the Milieu

  • -3.9 By

    An ancient and powerful civilization expands in the galaxy seeding its genetic material on the most promising planets suitable for life.

  • -2 My

    The Un'ie build a dense network of stargates that allow instant travel between them. Their civilization reaches its peak and then disappears. Only the gates and rare artifacts survive the advance of time.

  • 12,563

    On Earth, humans succeed for the first time in splitting the atom to harness nuclear energy.

  • 12,850

    After colonizing their home star system and beginning to move among the stars at sub-light speed, humans on Earth make contact with the Milieu civilization. Within a few decades they become minor members.

  • 17,236

    Humans have expanded across a considerable portion of Orion's Arm, colonizing thousands of star systems and moving further and further away from their homeworld. The journey from the outer fringes to Earth can take decades, even using the rare working stargates.
